Organization- Ministry of Labour and Employment

Establishment

  • Ministry of Labour & Social Welfare, 2038 BS
  • Ministry of Labour, 2052 BS
  • Ministry of Labour & Transport Management, 2057 BS
  • inistry of Labour & Employment, 2069

Objectives and Long Term vision of Ministry

  • Development of Pure Industrial Relationship
  • Ending Unemployment and Development of Productive and Qualitative Employment System
  • Child Labour Alleviation
  • Development of Safety, Managed and help based transportation system

Responsibilities of Ministry

  • Labour policy and Work completion
  • Study, investigation, data collection and verification of labour power and labour market.
  • Contact and relationship development of labour with national and international chambers and corporations
  • Relationship between Labour and management.
  • Help Employee and Labour supply.
  • Foreign Employments
  • Promotion, supply and organize vocational trainings
  • Training on child, women and disabled labours.
  • Trade unions
  • For social safety of Labour
  • Permission for Foreign Employees
  • Labour Administration and Management.
  • Bonus
  • Manage, organize Transportation (Air transport not included) Policies and Planning, Regulations and Work completions.
  • Manage, organize Transportation (Air transport not included) with other chambers and corporation
  • Relationship between Transportation (Air transport not included) with other related and managed international corporations
